# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Blockly Student OSS project a respectful and welcoming experience for everyone.

We value constructive discussion, thoughtful collaboration, and kindness.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in speech and actions
- Provide constructive feedback
- Assume good intent
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Follow GitHubâ€™s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Aggressive or hostile behavior in issues, pull requests, or discussions

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may result in being blocked from participating in the project.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening a GitHub issue or contacting the project maintainer directly.
